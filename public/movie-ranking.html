The Best Films According to Angelina

My top 3 films of all-time

1. The Life of Brian

Directed by Terry Jones, this Monty Python classic is a hilarious take on the life of Jesus Christ.

2. The Shawshank Redemption

Directed by Frank Darabont, this film is a moving story of friendship and hope.

3. The Godfather

Directed by Francis Ford Coppola, this film is a classic tale of power, family, and betrayal.
